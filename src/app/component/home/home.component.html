Who Are We?

FREE HEALTHCARE SERVICES FOR THOSE WHO CANNOT AFFORD

Transparent Hands Foundation US INC is a 501©(3) nonprofit organization registered in the USA. One of the largest crowdfunding platform in the healthcare sector of Pakistan. It offers a complete range of free healthcare services including medical and surgical treatments and free medical camps facilities to the underprivileged community of Pakistan. This online fundraising platform provides an online giving opportunity for donors from around the globe to donate online to needy patients in Pakistan while ensuring complete transparency. It also sets up free medical camps in the rural areas of Pakistan in which, free consultation, free medicines, free health awareness sessions, and free diagnostic tests facilities are also provided to deserving patients.

HEALTHCARE ISSUES IN PAKISTAN

More than 80 million people in Pakistan are living below the poverty line due to which they are unable to undergo proper health treatment if they suffer from any disease. The estimated number of surgery backlogs in Pakistan is in millions every year.

  • There are not many health insurance plans for the underprivileged community due to which, the majority of the people are unable to undergo medical or surgical treatment.
  • Beyond the cost of medical and surgical procedures, they have to pay for other expenses such as pre and post-operative expenses that include; medical tests, medications, and hospital stay charges. It is almost impossible for someone who earns less than $2 a day to pay a hefty amount for medical relief.
  • There is a lack of medical facilities and medical professionals in rural areas across Pakistan due to which, poverty-stricken families are unable to avail required treatment.
  • There are a limited number of beds in local hospitals which are not enough to cater to the healthcare needs of underprivileged communities in Pakistan.
  • Every second patient including a child is put on a long waiting list for free treatment and during the waiting period, the patient’s health further deteriorates. In most cases, patients expire until their turn comes for free treatment in government hospitals.
  • Private hospitals are out of the reach for lower-income class families due to the high costs of consultation fees and medical & surgical procedures.

Transparent Hands’ role in the community

COMMITTED TO PROVIDING FREE HEALTHCARE SERVICES TO PEOPLE IN NEED ACROSS PAKISTAN

Transparent Hands is a non-profit organization that accepts donations online and is tackling this major healthcare issue in Pakistan through its unique Crowdfunding platform. Transparent Hands is registered in USA EIN assigned: 47-3564801 as ‘Transparent Hands Foundation US, Inc.’ with tax exemption status 501(c) (3). Transparent Hands provides free medical and surgical treatment facilities to deserving patients across Pakistan in private hospitals with the help of donations from people around the globe. It also sets up free medical camps in rural areas of Pakistan in which free consultation, free medicines, and free diagnostic tests facility is given to the needy patients and their families. Donors from all over the world can use our online donations crowdfunding web portal and donate through 100% secure payment modes. They could select any patient, donate online for their treatment and receive regular feedback and updates until the patient is fully recovered. Our user-friendly online fundraising platform works as follows:

  • Select a campaign that you want to support.
  • Choose from a number of payment methods.
  • Donate any amount and get a tax exemption.
  • Get regular updates on the patient until he/she gets fully recovered.

HOW TRANSPARENT HANDS’ IS UNIQUE?

100% transparency model, donors are ensured that each penny they donate is spent on needy patients. We do this by uploading all the hospital bills and other documents of our patient after his/her surgery is successfully conducted or he/she undergoes complete medical treatment. The donor can see the exact amount that has been charged against the treatment of that particular patient for which he has donated.

OUR IMPACT ON THE PAKISTANI COMMUNITY

Transparent Hands is currently dealing with a number of surgeries including orthopedic, cardiac, neurology, gynecology, and other general surgeries. Cochlear Implant surgery for example cost around $8,000 to $9,000. Transparent Hands provides it for free to its patients. It is also providing free medical procedures including Angioplasty, Hepatitis C Treatment,  Chemotherapy, MRI, Endoscopy, and Rehabilitation procedures for the disabled.Transparent Hands is focusing on reaching out to the masses throughout the country who don’t have access to basic healthcare facilities by setting up free medical camps. It intends to expand to other areas of Pakistan where the poverty-stricken community has no means of getting better healthcare facilities through telehealth facilities.

Transparent Hands’ mission is to conduct free life-saving surgeries/medical treatments for poor people all across Pakistan through its transparent crowdfunding online healthcare platform.
